About

Mechanical Engineer | Energy Systems | Optimization

Master's student in Energy Science and Technology at ETH Zürich, with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Cyprus.

My work focuses on the modeling and optimization of multi-energy systems, with particular emphasis on heating and cooling technologies. I am especially interested in developing efficient, data-driven solutions for energy systems that integrate thermodynamics, optimization, and real-world engineering constraints.

Through academic and research experience across ETH Zürich, University of Cyprus, Texas A&M University, and industry, I have developed strong analytical and computational skills applied to complex energy problems.
